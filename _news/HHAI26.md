**Chiara, Giacomo and Tommaso at HHAI 2026**

Last week, our group members Chiara Natali, Giacomo Zamprogno and Tommaso Zendron presented at the [Conference on Hybrid Human Artificial Intelligence](https://hhai-conference.org/2026/) in Brussels. 
Chiara organised the [3rd workshop on Frictional Design in Hybrid Human Systems](https://sites.google.com/view/frictional-ai/home), and presented her short paper ["Emergence in Human-AI Systems"](https://ebooks.iospress.nl/doi/10.3233/FAIA260497), where Hybrid Intelligence is framed as the modern-day successor to early research on cybernetics, HCI and CSCW, and emergence is presented as a methodological constraint for HI. At the same workshop Giacomo presented the abstract "Beyond the Black Box: Designing Frictional Interfaces for Trust Calibration and Appropriate Reliance in Scientific LLMs" co-authored by Giacomo, Laura Capella and Ilaria Tiddi. Tommaso presented at the [workshop on Hybrid Intelligence for Health Care](https://hi4healthcare.github.io) the abstract "AI as a Mediator of Information: a Consent-Aware Team Design Pattern for Appropriate Trust in Healthcare" co-authored by Tommaso, Josephine Mélot Chesnel, Berke Yazan and Emma M. van Zoelen.
