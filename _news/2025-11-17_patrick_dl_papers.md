The proceedings can be found at the following <a href="https://ceur-ws.org/Vol-4091/">link</a>.

Here follow Patrick and colleagues' contributions: 

- <a href="https://ceur-ws.org/Vol-4091/paper07.pdf">**Towards Conceptual Clustering in EL with Simulation Graphs (Ruud van Bakel, Michael Cochez, Patrick Koopmann)**</a> - a collaboration with the LnR group of the VU on unsupervised learning of description logic concepts
- <a href="https://ceur-ws.org/Vol-4091/paper25.pdf">**Why not? Developing ABox Abduction Beyond Repairs (Anselm Haak, Patrick Koopmann, Yasir Mahmood, Anni-Yasmin Turhan)**</a> - collaboration with Paderborn, on explaining and repairing missing entailments in inconsistent knowledge bases
- <a href="https://ceur-ws.org/Vol-4091/paper20.pdf">**Concrete Domains Meet Expressive Cardinality Restrictions in Description Logics (Franz Baader, Stefan Borgwardt, Filippo De Bortoli, Patrick Koopmann)**</a> - collaboration with Dresden, abstract to an accepted paper at CADE (still waiting for it to appear on the Springer proceedings), this is on combining numerical reasoning with logical reasoning in a new description logic
