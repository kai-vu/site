From march the 10th to march the 11th the KAI spring outing took place at the Hunningaheerd in Beerta, Groningen. For two days we shared our space in a beautiful maison. We presented our personal researches in creative ways, and we explored the connections among our researches, including past projects and future directions. A fruitful team-building experience that helped us understand the expertise of each other, the challenges everyone is currently tackling, and the solutions being explored.
